Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for planning a retreat?

expand_more

We recommend starting the planning process at least 3-4 months in advance, especially for groups larger than 50 people. However, we can accommodate expedited timelines depending on the destination.

Do you handle international retreats?

expand_more

Absolutely. We have partnerships with venues in over 30 countries. We handle all logistics including flights, visas assistance, and local transportation.

Is there a minimum group size?

expand_more

Our services are optimized for groups of 10 or more. For smaller executive teams, we offer specialized "Micro-Retreat" packages.

From Adrenaline to Zen:
The WTC Story

World Travel Consortium was founded on a simple premise: Growth doesn't happen in the comfort zone.

In 2008, our founders—a former corporate executive and a professional mountaineer—realized that traditional corporate retreats were broken. Golf courses and hotel ballrooms weren't creating the deep bonds needed for modern high-performance teams.

We set out to create experiences that matter. Whether it's scaling a peak in the Andes to teach resilience or practicing silent meditation in Kyoto to foster clarity, our itineraries are meticulously crafted to align with your organizational goals. Today, we are the trusted partner for Fortune 500 companies seeking to elevate their teams.

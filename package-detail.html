Overview

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush and spiritual awakening with our private Everest Base Camp Helicopter Tour. Bypass the grueling weeks of trekking and soar directly into the heart of the Khumbu region, witnessing panoramic views of four of the world's six tallest peaks.

Designed for corporate retreats and high-end travelers, this extreme logistics package ensures seamless transfers, oxygen availability at high altitudes, and a champagne breakfast at the Hotel Everest View—the highest placed hotel in the world.
